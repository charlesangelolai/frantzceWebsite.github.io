About Me

Frantz was a musician from Orlando, Florida. He first started as a euphonium player, who allowed music to be his place of comfort. As he grew more into the realm of music, he began to play the trombone, and started rapping. After graduating high school, he performed in a theaterâ€™s orchestra, playing the trombone. Frantz fell in love with being a part of the theater. He decided to move to New York to pursue acting. He graduated from the New York Conservatory for the Dramatic Arts. While he was in acting school, he got involved in a parkour gym. The parkour gym introduced him to new skills, such as: Parkour, tumbling, trampoline (with and without the wall), break-dancing, and bar calisthenics.

Dance

Frantz joined the dance community as a breakdancer. He used elements from his free running and tumbling to make him a creative bboy. Every step, every freeze, every movement made him addicted to do more and more. Seeking for growth, Frantz studied under Kid Kiba, Chun Venus, and Bryan Longchamp (IronBry).

Free Running

Frantz is a parkour free runner and he is a parkour/ ninja warrior coach. He started training with ParkourNYC in 2015 and worked his way up to be a professional free runner.

Lifestyle

Frantz loves videogames and anime. Most of his inspiration in his movements and sketches comes from playing console games, Naruto, or One Piece.
